Freedom But At What Cost

You haven't spotted the creature yet, but unknown to you it has you in its sights. The boat starts rocking more and more, eventually
getting to the point where the waves could not be causing it. Then you notice a scraping noise as the tentacle of the creature slams into the
side of the boat leaving a huge gash. You have to make the decision to run across the deck and grab the shotgun. You shoot once, nothing.
Twice, nothing. Then finally the third shot hits. The creature lets out a terrible scream and diverts its coarse.

The further you get from the scene, the more the storm calms. Eventually there is nothing but calm seas again.

You have escaped for now but the creature still lurks in the deep, waiting for its next victim
