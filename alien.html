Alien Invasion

By Riley Rubin

12/22/1976, Utah, USA

It was a quiet morning like any other. I woke up, ate breakfast
and went to work. Little did I know, today would change
history. I worked at an observatory mainly focused on
studying other planets. Just before lunch, me and some of my
coworkers noticed something very strange on Mars that none
of us could explain. There was a series of flashes, almost
identical to a rocket launching, over and over coming from the
surface of the red planet. Two at a time, a total of sixteen of
these flashes happened, then nothing for about five minutes.
What looked like three lasers streamed towards Earth at the
speed of light with little to no warning. They were here in a
few minutes and stopped instantly upon reaching orbit.

The Encounter

The sixteen objects were sitting perfectly still, looking like
small blue lights in the sky, barely visible to the naked eye,
but upon closer inspection through the telescope were what
looked like spaceships. They were triangular with three
rocket engines on the back and a blue light centered on the
bottom. They were all different sizes and in a formation.
Two giant ones in the middle, four slightly smaller ones
surrounding them, and ten very small ones scattered around
in an organized pattern around the group. One of the big
ships launched some kind of drop pod that plummeted
towards the ground in a ball of fire. It landed on the side of a
back road just out of town. Some of us ran outside, jumped in
our cars and started racing down the road. When we arrived
at the scene there was a huge crowd of cars and people
stopped staring at the object. I made my way through the
crowd until I saw it. There was a strange arrowhead shaped
object. It was shiny but not very reflective, like polished
aluminum but darker and it had a light blue light on the top.
A line of light started at the bottom and slowly moved to the
top then two doors opened slowly, revealing the creature inside.

Communication

An acidic feeling filled the air as the creature stepped out of
the pod without a scratch, despite the rough landing. As it
touched the grass, it began to die and the scent of burnt
leather was pointing in the direction towards it. The alien was
tall with four arms and big hands, it had four eyes and light
grey patterned skin that looked very dry. It began looking back
and fourth, scanning the crowd of concerned people.

The Start Of The End

The county sheriff was among the crowd, previously too
stunned to speak, he stood there. He eventually made a
decision without thinking, one that would change
everything. He drew his sidearm and aimed it at the
creature. It immediately made a noise that sounded like a
viking war horn and threw something at the sheriff killing
him instantly. The ships from above descended through the
atmosphere and started opening fire upon every single
person they could see. I was thinking quickly and dove
under one of the cars next to me as I witnessed mass
destruction follow around in every direction. All I could hear
was screaming, gunfire, and explosions. I also heard the
familiar sound of fighter jets screaming overhead. I peaked
out for a second to see one firing missiles at the spaceships,
doing absolutely no damage, then getting struck from the sky
and plummeting to the ground in a ball of fire. I stayed still
under the car until all nearby noises stopped, then climbed out
and what I saw was something I couldnâ€™t comprehend.
Everything around me was destroyed, collapsed, and on fire.
It looked like there was a war that lasted many years, but it
happened in only hours.
