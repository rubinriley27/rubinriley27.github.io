My Favorite Cars

My all time favorite car is the 1963 Corvette Stingray Split Window. In my opinion, this is the most
beautiful car in the world and nobody can change my mind. My favorite part of this car is the rear
window design. It is a boat tail design with two pieces of curved glass and a split down the middle.


My second favorite car is the 1971 Buick Riviera. There is not a single part of this car that looks
imperfect. This car also shares a boat tail rear window with the Stingray, but it has a very cool front
end as well. The side profile looks super sleek and sporty, as it should.


My third favorite car is the 1957 Buick Skylark Convertible. This car has the most beautiful side profile
of any 50s car I know. Most cars from the 40s and 50s have over dramatic body lines that make them look
almost cartoonish, but Buick did it almost perfect. Its not too tall, too round, or too long. Its just perfect.
